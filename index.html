Upper/Lower 4‑Day Strength & Hypertrophy

Progressive overload • Rest 2–3m compounds, 45–90s accessories
Week 1
Warm-up
  • 5–10 min light cardio
  • Dynamic mobility for joints involved
  • 2–3 ramp-up sets for first compound
Cool-down
  • Light walking/bike 3–5 min
  • Stretch worked muscle groups 5–8 min
Week Notes
Tip: Hit the top of a rep range for all sets with solid form → increase weight next session.
